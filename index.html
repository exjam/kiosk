Kiosk is an independent publisher and temporary, mobile bookshop with a focus on self-published zines & artist books from young creatives. It is a platform designed to give a voice to emerging artists, illustrators, designers and small press publishers by promoting their work and making it available for sale, as well as publishing titles on their behalf.

Kiosk first started as a temporary bookshop held inside The Peckham Pelican, as a part of the Art Licks Weekend 2014, selling books, zines and artwork from a wide variety of independent and talented contributors.
