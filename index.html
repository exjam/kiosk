Kiosk is an independent temporary bookshop with a focus on self-publishing, based inside café & bar, The Peckham Pelican and held on the occasion of the Art Licks Weekend 2014. It is a platform designed to give a voice to emerging artists, illustrators, designers and small press publishers by promoting their work and making it available for sale.

Items for sale will include self-published books, zines, magazines, prints and ceramics from a wide variety of independent and talented contributors. Kiosk is a non-profit project, with profits going back to the shop's contributors.

We have also commissioned a select group of new artists & designers to contribute a zine exclusively for Kiosk. These will be exhibited & sold for the duration of the weekend as well as afterwards.

Kiosk will be open from Thursday 2 October – Sunday 5 October, with an opening night on Thursday evening at the Pelican to celebrate the launch of the bookshop from 7.30pm–11pm.
