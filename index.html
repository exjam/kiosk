Kiosk is a London based independent publisher with a focus on self-published zines & artist books from young creatives. It is a platform designed to give a voice to emerging artists, illustrators, designers by promoting their work and collaborating with them to produce publications.

Kiosk launched with a temporary bookshop during the Art Licks Weekend 2014, selling books, zines and artwork from a wide variety of independent and talented contributors. We currently curate the irregularly scheduled Kiosk Independent Publishers Fair; check our Events to see previous and upcoming fairs. Kiosk is run solely by graphic designer, Nick Greenbank.

You can find selected titles stocked at Actual Source (UT, USA), Beach London (UK), Good Press (Glasgow, UK), Printed Matter (NY, USA), Village Bookstore (Leeds, UK), Whitechapel Gallery (London, UK), On Sundays (JP), Strange Store (JP).
